SINIGANG

Description

Sinigang is a popular Filipino dish that is characterized by its sour taste. It is
typically made by simmering meat, such as pork or shrimp, with vegetables and
tamarind or other souring agents to create a flavorful and comforting soup.

Ingredients

  • 500 grams of Meat
  • Sinigang Mix packet
  • 8 cups of Water
  • 1 medium-sized Onion, sliced
  • 2 medium-sized Tomatoes, quartered
  • 200 grams of Green beans (sitaw), cut into 2-inch pieces
  • 150 grams of Radishes (labanos), sliced
  • 1 medium-sized Eggplant, sliced
  • 1 bunch of Kangkong leaves (water spinach)
  • 150 grams of Okra
  • 2-3 Long chili peppers (siling pansigang)
  • Salt
  • Fish sauce (patis)
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Boil 8 cups of water in a pot.
  2. Add the meat and cook until tender.
  3. Add sliced onions, quartered tomatoes, and the sinigang mix. Simmer for a few minutes.
  4. Add green beans, radishes, eggplant, okra, and sliced long chili peppers. Cook until tender.
  5. Season with salt and fish sauce to taste. Add pepper for extra flavor.
  6. Add kangkong leaves and cook for a minute until wilted.
  7. Remove from heat and serve hot with steamed rice.

Enjoy your delicious Sinigang!
