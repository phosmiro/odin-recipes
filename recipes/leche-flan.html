Leche Flan

Ingredients

  • 5 egg yolks
  • 1/2 can (7 oz) of condensed milk
  • 1/2 can (6 oz) of evaporated milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 6 tablespoons of white sugar for caramel

Steps

  1. Mix the egg yolks, condensed milk, evaporated milk, and vanilla extract.
  2. Caramelize the white sugar in a saucepan and coat the bottom of a mold with it.
  3. Pour the egg mixture into the mold and steam for 30-40 minutes.
  4. Cool, refrigerate for 2 hours, then invert onto a plate to serve.

Enjoy your Leche Flan with its creamy goodness!
